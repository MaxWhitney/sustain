Public Projects

  Apereo OAE - A global network for academic collaboration, OAE is fully open and created and backed by universities. I chaired the Technical Review Board in 2012, was a member of the Product Council and wrote an O'Reilly book on OAE management and customization. I remain so proud of my team and the success of this cross-university collaboration in building open source software to satisfy our own teaching and research needs.
  Open Referral Human Services Data Specification - The Human Services Data Specification (HSDS) is Open Referral's core data exchange format. HSDS is designed to support the publication of open, interoperable community resource directory data. I documented the underlying logical model implied by the API, clarifying and assisting in the resolution of ambiguities therein.
  NYC Resistor 2000 - NYCR2000 is a text adventure game (with limited quantities of adventure, but lots of exploration) based on the physical space occupied by NYC Resistor circa 2017. Resistor is a hacker collective in downtown Brooklyn which meets regularly to share knowledge, hack on projects together, and build community.
  Crypto Party - CryptoParty is a decentralized movement with events happening all over the world. The goal is to pass on knowledge about protecting yourself in the digital space. I frequently present or facilitate at local Crypto Party events, and sometimes even at Verso Books, UnionDocs and Harvard's Kennedy School.
  Black Girls Code - Sometimes I get to teach kids to code at Black Girls Code. We've done a web page in a day, a weekend mobile phone app, a Sphero robot maze follower. You can donate to BGC, buy some swag or sign up to volunteer.
